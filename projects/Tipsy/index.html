About

Tipsy is a team project aimed at designing an integrated application for users who wish to purchase liquors online and make cocktails on their own. We want to help our users to easily access to alcohol-related items and knowledges by creating a platform that provides comprehensive products and services.

Domain Research

According to an industry research firm from The International Wine and Spirits Record, alcohol delivery service is a rapidly developing market that became particularly popular after the pandemic, they predicts “the alcohol eCommerce market will still increase by 42% this year (2021) to reach $24 billion.”

According to Drizly’s report, there are also some engaging trends in this area that could create more marketplaces. For example, “products for health-conscious shoppers increased by 57%, at a time when 90% of retailers on Drizly now stock non-alcoholic beer, wine, and other alternatives, and share of such products on Drizly are up 120% since 2020”. On the other hand, there is also a trend that combines alcohol delivery with other features like groceries and cocktails.

Against the backdrop of the growing popularity of home entertainment in the post-pandemic era, our goal is to seize new growth opportunities in the liquor delivery market and win in the fierce competition for products.

Interview Synthesis

In the alcohol-related market, we found out that there is still not a provider offering a comprehensive experience which results in users had to use multiple applications and websites for different occasions and situations.

What we want to do is to utilize the development of alcohol delivery market as an opportunity to establish an integrated one-stop platform for alcohol-related services.

On the home page, we designed a comprehensive menu which includes a veriaty of categories. We also added some recommending categories according to users' past order the rating of products to help users easily find what they want.

In the search bar, we integrated the scanner feature, which can help users quickly know about a product by scanning the lebel on the bottle.

The product page has very detailed description and information, including rating and comments from other users. This can help users make decision, and at the same time, community building can also help increase user engagement and stickiness.

The cocktail page also have comprehensive categories where user can look for recipes according to base liquor and type of cocktail.

The recipes can automatically change based on units and serving numbers. Also, users can easily find all ingredients and equipment they need and can directly add the whole package to cart.
